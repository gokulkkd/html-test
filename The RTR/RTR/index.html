THE RTR-S CERTIFICATE

Dear Trainers,
We’re getting very excited to complete the launch of the RTR-Sociometrics Certificate Program. We are grateful to have an incredible team of Trainers and look forward to giving you our full support. In this letter, we’d like to outline opportunities for you to become highlighted as a trainer, host your own training, and leverage the optional support now available to you through TPN.health. As we begin publicizing the Certificate to treatment centers and clinicians, we want to ensure that you are aware of your options as Trainers.

Getting Started:

1. Onboarding: To officially join us, please onboard to the RTR-S organization page on TPN.health.

2. Meet the Trainers: For a feature in our “Meet the Trainers” section on the Welcome Page, submit your profile via this Jotform link. As we continue our launch to treatment centers, this is a great opportunity for exposure and to provide direct contact information if desired.

3. Resources: To help you get started, we’ve organized a shared folder of informational and promotional materials, which will be updated as additional assets are developed. View the RTR-S Facilitator Tool Kit. Here is the RTR-S manual for you to refer to. This is only for your use as a trainer, please do not share it as this would constitute a copyright infringement by you as a trainer. It will be available to the students you onboard to become certificate holders and should not be shown to them in advance nor to anyone else.

How Trainings Will Work:

Option 1: Organize an In-Person Training with Support from Tian and TPN.health!
If you’d like to be entrepreneurial, here's a great option.

1. Your Role:

  • Supply the space
  • Set the registration fee as you see fit
  • Educate and engage. Provide the 2-day basic training following the RTRS protocol (level I) or training to fulfill the remaining 3-days in psychodrama/sociometry (level II & III)
  • Collect 70% of revenue

2. Tian x TPN.health’s Role:

  • CE accreditation for all major behavioral health boards, as applicable (ASWB, APA, NADAAC, NBCC, NYSED, CAMFT+).
  • Registration page creation, attendance tracking, streamlined CE certificates, plus access to pre and post-event data insights.
  • Marketing support: Promotional assets and co-marketing efforts to Tian’s and TPN.health’s combined email lists of 170k+ clinicians. Email campaigns, TPN.health newsfeed and social media shoutouts. This is exclusively for when doing RTR-S specific trainings.
  • 30% of revenue collected by Tian/TPN.health (a minimum may apply). We designed this split to be as favorable to trainers as possible to encourage you to consider this option. As long as you are onboarding people from your training to the certificate path, we will be able to keep this split going.

Option 2: We Bring Training Opportunities to You!
As we share the Certificate Program with Treatment Centers, we are hearing from programs interested in bringing a trainer to their site.

  • Tian Dayton and Sarah Duke, Chief Development Officer at TPN.health, will meet with interested organizations to connect them with trainers.
  • We will act as an intermediary, getting in touch to check your availability and willingness to provide the training.
  • We negotiate a fee that works for all parties. The goal is to secure a minimum of $2,000 per day, per trainer, plus travel expenses, allowing $1,500 to go to you, the Trainer, and $500 (25%) to Tian Dayton/TPN.health.
  • TPN.health handles CEs, attendance tracking, evaluations/certificates, and customer support.

Option 3: Host In-Person Training Independently!
Already connected with a treatment center or contacted by one directly? You are welcome to proceed with facilitating training independently.

  • You may facilitate RTRS trainings at facilities, or for the general public without any assistance or fees required from Tian Dayton or TPN.health.
  • We ask that you please hand out or email participants onboarding materials so that trainees learn how to get an RTR-S certificate if they choose to.
    • Please refer to the RTR-S Facilitator Toolkit for these materials.
  • TPN.health’s support is completely optional. You may contact TPN.health for assistance with treatment center negotiations and CE management. Should you elect support from TPN.health, please refer to the compensation models provided above.
    • Community wide trainings where you set the ticket price: refer to the compensation model in Option 1.
    • For staff training at facilities: refer to Option 2.

Option 4: Host Virtual RTR-S Training on TPN.health!
You have the option to develop RTR-S specific virtual training to fulfill this program’s requirements with support from TPN.health

  • Up to one-third of the required psychodrama hours for levels II and III can be virtual, but they must be live and specific to RTRS, e.g., your integration of RTRS into another approach, or your RTRS adaptation for specific populations.
  • For services and support, please refer to Option 1. TPN.health will handle registration, CE, and will also provide a moderator for live virtual training.
  • Contact TPN.health to discuss course development through a revenue share between Tian and TPN.health.
