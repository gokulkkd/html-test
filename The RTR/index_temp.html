THE RTR-S CERTIFICATE

Dear Trainers,
We’re getting very excited to complete the launch of the RTR-Sociometrics Certificate Program. We are grateful to have an incredible team of Trainers and look forward to giving you our full support. In this letter, we’d like to outline opportunities for you to become highlighted as a trainer, host your own training, and leverage the optional support now available to you through TPN.health. As we begin publicizing the Certificate to treatment centers and clinicians, we want to ensure that you are aware of your options as Trainers.

Getting Started:

  1. Onboarding: To officially join us, please onboard to the RTR-S organization page on TPN.health.
  2. Meet the Trainers: For a feature in our “Meet the Trainers” section on the Welcome Page, submit your profile via this Jotform link. As we continue our launch to treatment centers, this is a great opportunity for exposure and to provide direct contact information if desired.
  3. Resources: help you get started, we’ve organized a shared folder of informational and promotional materials, which will be updated as additional assets are developed. View the RTR-S Facilitator Tool Kit. Here is the RTR-S manual for you to refer to. This is only for your use as a trainer, please do not share it as this would constitute a copyright infringement by you as a trainer. It will be available to the students you onboard to become certificate holders and should not be shown to them in advance nor to anyone else.
