HISTORY OF HOW IT ALL STARTED

Sandy Gunn

December 7, 2015

It all started with painting a portrait of this wolf at an art workshop put on by Lesley Harrison, a famous artist. When I came home there were no wolves to paint. But there many Pomeranian and other dogs which are just like tiny wolves. I have been painting dogs in ever since. Yes, I was trained by Lesley Harrison â€¦who receives between $15,000 and $35,000 for each of her portraits. She has become world famous and has books she has written on her techniques. I have hundreds of pastels in every conceivable color. Each pastel can cost up to $5, so I have a very lot of money invested in my art supplies.
